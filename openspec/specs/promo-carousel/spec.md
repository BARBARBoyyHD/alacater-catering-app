# Promo Carousel

The promo carousel capability displays promotional banners in a horizontal auto-scrolling carousel.

## Requirements

### REQ-1: Banner Display
The system SHALL render a horizontal carousel of promo banners with:
- Fixed height (200px)
- Full-width images with rounded corners (borderRadius: 20)
- Text overlay on the banner (promo title, discount info)

### REQ-2: Pagination Dots
The carousel SHALL display pagination dots at the bottom:
- Active dot: elongated white pill (width: 24px)
- Inactive dots: small semi-transparent white circles (width: 8px)
- Centered horizontally

### REQ-3: Banner Interaction
Each banner SHALL be tappable and navigate to a promo detail view.

### REQ-4: Spacing
The banner carousel SHALL have 16px margin on all sides within the home screen.
