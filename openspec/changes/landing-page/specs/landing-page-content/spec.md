## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Landing Page Content Structure
The system SHALL provide distinct sections for the landing page content as outlined in the backlog.

#### Scenario: All sections are present
- **WHEN** a user visits the landing page
- **THEN** the page displays the hero section, features showcase, provider signup CTA, customer app download section, testimonials, FAQ, and footer.

### Requirement: Hero Section
The hero section SHALL prominently display the Alacater value proposition and a primary call to action.

#### Scenario: Hero content is displayed
- **WHEN** the hero section loads
- **THEN** it shows a compelling headline, sub-headline, and a clear CTA button (e.g., "Get Started", "Sign Up").

### Requirement: Features Showcase
The features showcase section SHALL highlight key benefits of Alacater for both customers and providers.

#### Scenario: Feature details are visible
- **WHEN** the features showcase section is viewed
- **THEN** it displays distinct cards or sections for each key feature with brief descriptions.

### Requirement: Provider Signup CTA
A clear call-to-action SHALL be present to encourage providers to sign up.

#### Scenario: Provider signup CTA is visible and clickable
- **WHEN** the landing page is viewed
- **THEN** a prominent button or link for provider signup is visible and directs users to the signup flow.

### Requirement: Customer App Download Section
A section SHALL be dedicated to encouraging customers to download the mobile application.

#### Scenario: App download links are displayed
- **WHEN** the customer app download section is viewed
- **THEN** it displays links or buttons for app store downloads (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play Store).

### Requirement: Testimonials/Reviews Section
The landing page SHALL feature testimonials or reviews to build trust and credibility.

#### Scenario: Testimonials are displayed
- **WHEN** the testimonials section is viewed
- **THEN** it shows quotes from satisfied users or providers, potentially with names or images.

### Requirement: FAQ Section
An FAQ section SHALL be included to address common user questions.

#### Scenario: FAQ items are displayed
- **WHEN** the FAQ section is viewed
- **THEN** it displays a list of questions and their corresponding answers, potentially with accordions for answers.

### Requirement: Footer
A consistent footer SHALL be present across the landing page.

#### Scenario: Footer content is displayed
- **WHEN** any part of the landing page is viewed
- **THEN** the footer is visible and contains essential links (e.g., About Us, Contact, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service).
